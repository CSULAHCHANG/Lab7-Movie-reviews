Dream Team Assemble!

From comics to movie, the representation of our favorite characters portrays with their best casts, leading to the best fictional universe EVER.

Synopsis

The Avengers

“When Thor's evil brother, Loki (Tom Hiddleston), gains access to the unlimited power of the energy cube called the Tesseract, Nick Fury (Samuel L. Jackson), director of S.H.I.E.L.D., initiates a superhero recruitment effort to defeat the unprecedented threat to Earth.

Joining Fury's "dream team" are Iron Man (Robert Downey Jr.), Captain America (Chris Evans), the Hulk (Mark Ruffalo), Thor (Chris Hemsworth), the Black Widow (Scarlett Johansson) and Hawkeye (Jeremy Renner).”

Avengers: Infinity War

“Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk and the rest of the Avengers unite to battle their most powerful enemy yet -- the evil Thanos. On a mission to collect all six Infinity Stones, Thanos plans to use the artifacts to inflict his twisted will on reality.

The fate of the planet and existence itself has never been more uncertain as everything the Avengers have fought for has led up to this moment.”

Avengers: Endgame

“Adrift in space with no food or water, Tony Stark sends a message to Pepper Potts as his oxygen supply starts to dwindle. Meanwhile, the remaining Avengers --

Thor, Black Widow, Captain America and Bruce Banner -- must figure out a way to bring back their vanquished allies for an epic showdown with Thanos -- the evil demigod who decimated the planet and the universe.”

Reviews

This movie is where the dream team is all began, as well as leading Marvel universe into phase 4. Teasing audience with massive storyline from the comics leading up to the big finale.

Part 1 of phase 4 ending of Marvel universe, a perfect film showing both sides of the story. From villain Thanos to Avengers Iron Man all the characters seamlessly bonded together.

Part 2 of phase 4 Marvel universe, a masterpiece that fulfill audiences’ expectation after the wild ending from Infinity War that no one expected. Although this is an end for a lot of characters, however it grants the Marvel universe the ability to move into phase 5 and their upcoming stories.
